Accessible Examples

These are specific examples of accessible code for use across the website. They each cover:

  1. What the accessibility requirements are.
  2. A method of meeting those requirements.

With good knowledge of accessibility you can come up with different methods of meeting the same requirements, but they should be thoroughly tested.

NB: These are not designed, production ready pieces of code (yet), they are intended to cover the accessibility techniques needed.

  • Show/hide content
